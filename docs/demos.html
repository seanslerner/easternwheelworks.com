    Reserve A Bike

    Reserve online for pick-up at Eastern Wheelworks in La Grande

    Use the form below to check real-time availability and complete your reservation online. Please review and sign our rental policies when booking your reservation!

    What to bring

    Demo bikes come with flat pedals. If you prefer to use your own pedals, bring them and we’re happy to install them for you.

    We offer helmets with bike rental, free of charge. All demo riders must wear a helmet. We strongly recommend you bring all your normal riding gear, protection, and accessories that you normally ride with. Traveling without your ride kit? We have gear & protection for sale at the shop!

    We don’t have bike racks to rent. If you are planning on transporting a bike, you must have a way to properly transport it, store it, and secure it.

    In order to demo a bicycle at Eastern Wheelworks, please have your ID and Credit Card with you upon bike pick up. You must sign a liability waiver. Riders under the age of 18 must have a parent present to sign a liability waiver.

    Cancellations & Charges

    We try to be as understanding and accommodating of circumstances as possible with our rentals and reservations. We know ‘it happens’ and aim to work with riders, however:

    Riders will be charged for any and all damages or losses that occur to any bicycles, gear, or parts during your rental.

    If you would like to modify or cancel your reservation, you must call and chat with someone on our team or email sean@easternwheelworks.com before 3:00 PM the day before your reservation.

    Cancellations after the aforementioned cutoff will be charged for the full rental.

    Returns & Timing

    Rentals must be returned by 4pm (when the shop closes) on the last day of their rental or you will be charged for an additional rental day.

    Returns on Sunday - Tuesday must be scheduled ahead of time and confirmed by us.

    Ride Before You Buy Program

    Who doesn’t like to try a bike on familiar trails before buying? Turn your dollars spent on demos into credit towards your new bike.

    A rider can apply up to $500 in demo credit toward a bike up to 10% of a bike’s MSRP. *Does not apply to sale bikes.
