    Service Your Fork

    Please note, listed rates are for labor only, they do not include parts (seal kits, damper shaft, shims, etc) - This will vary from fork to fork

    50 Hour Service
    $60

    What's included in your service:

    • Thorough cleaning and inspection of fork exterior for damage and wear. Check for proper function
    • Drop fork lowers, clean and inspect stanchions and lower legs for wear
    • Replace dust wipers and foam rings. Lube bushings and seals.
    • Fork reassembly and fresh oil in lowers.
    • Previous damper and spring settings restored
    • If bike is here, re-install fork on bike. Includes brake caliper alignment & headset preload. Torque to factory spec.

    125 Hour Service
    $130

    What's included in your service:

    • Thorough cleaning and inspection of fork exterior for damage and wear. Check for proper function
    • Drop fork lowers, clean and inspect stanchions and lower legs for wear
    • Replace dust wipers and foam rings. Lube bushings and seals.
    • Complete damper disassembly. Inspect damper components (valves, shims, seal head, springs, etc) for wear or damage, replace accordingly. Replace all o-rings and glide rings.
    • Damper reassembly (bleed damper with vacuum pump if applicable)
    • Airspring disassembly, replacing all o-rings and glide rings. Lube and reassemble.
    • Fork reassembly and fresh oil in lowers.
    • Previous damper and spring settings restored
    • If bike is here, re-install fork on bike. Includes brake caliper alignment & headset preload. Torque to factory spec.

    Service Your Shock

    Please note, listed rates are for labor only, they do not include parts (seal kits, damper shaft, shims, etc) - This will vary from shock to shock

    50 Hour Service
    $60

    What's included in your service:

    • Thorough cleaning and inspection of shock exterior for damage and wear. Check for proper function
    • Replace seals and glide rings (including air can if applicable)
    • Fresh oil and re-assembly
    • Previous damper and spring settings restored
    • New shock DU bushings and eyelet hardware replaced if worn or upon request
    • If bike is here, re-install shock on bike and torque shock hardware to spec
    • NOTE: Fox Float X2 can only be fully rebuilt due to its design.

    125 Hour Service
    $130

    What's included in your service:

    • Thorough cleaning and inspection of shock exterior for damage and wear. Check for proper function
    • Complete disassembly and teardown of shock body and internals. Isolate every component of the shock, nothing goes untouched (shim stacks, bearing assembly, base valve, stanchions, damper piston, adjuster assemblies, etc)
    • Inspect damper components for wear or damage, replace components as necessary
    • Replace all seals, o-rings, glide rings, and bushings
    • Lube components and re-assemble
    • Bleed damper with vacuum pump
    • Dyno test for proper damper function
    • Previous damper and spring settings restored
    • New shock DU bushings and eyelet hardware if worn or upon request
    • If bike is here, re-install shock on bike and torque shock hardware to spec

    Suspension Service Warranty

    Fork and shock service comes with a 60 day warranty on workmanship or o-ing failure. Warranty does not cover external factors such as poor shock design or frame designs that put extra stress on the shock. Examples include but are not limited to: Fox Float X2s, strut mounted shocks, trunnion mount shocks, RockShox 32mm chassis forks.
